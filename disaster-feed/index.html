How to read this

Every row is a real FEMA public-assistance project worksheet obligated in the last 120 days — federal $ is the amount FEMA has committed, not spent yet. Bars above are dollar totals, not project counts; a handful of huge utility rebuilds can outweigh dozens of small ones. Click any state or category bar to filter the table below it.
