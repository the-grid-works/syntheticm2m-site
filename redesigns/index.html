10 design directions

Click any card to open the full site in that style. Every one has the same content — products, how-it-works, capabilities, contact — in a totally different skin.

R01

Swiss Minimal

White, black type, one red accent, brutal typographic grid. Serious and expensive-looking.

 R02

Luxury Magazine

Cream, elegant serif chapters, gold hairlines, drop caps. Feels like a private firm.

 R03

Neo-Brutalist

Yellow, thick black borders, hard shadows, buttons that squish. Loud and memorable.

 R04

Pastel Glass

Soft drifting gradients, frosted cards, dreamy floating motion. Friendly and modern.

 R05

CRT Terminal

Phosphor green console with scanlines — the whole site is a boot sequence typing itself.

 R06

Claymorphic

Puffy soft-3D cards, squishy buttons, morphing blobs. Playful, approachable.

 R07

Enterprise SaaS

Stripe-style: white, slate, one violet accent, crisp borders, counting stats. Investor-safe.

 R08

Synthwave

Neon sunset, chrome headline, endless perspective grid. Maximum vibe, maximum Tron.

 R09

Noir Mono

Black and white with enormous type and one acid accent. Dramatic, high-fashion.

 R10

Nature-Tech

Forest green + sand, breathing blobs, vines that draw themselves. Calm growth story.
